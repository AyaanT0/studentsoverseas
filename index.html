Who are we?

Students Over Seas is a student-led non-profit organization which works to bring awareness to the day-to-day issues of Ontario's international university students and help solve them. We're not backed by any corporation, so instead we leverage our audience on our website, Instagram, and Facebook to help raise awareness and bring change in issues such as inflated tuition prices, financial problems, the struggle of affordable housing, and language and cultural barriers for international students.
